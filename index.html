About Me

Decisions, decisions, that's what life is about. About six years ago, I made one of the hardest decisions of my life. I chose to leave my thriving career and focus my time and energy on cultivating a foundation for my family. Boy, did they thrive! I know that I made the right decision all those years ago, and I regret nothing. Fast forward to 2022, everyone is in school, everyone has a focus, and everyone is pretty content. Except me. I feel that familiar pull to create something, build something, and challenge myself. It is with no further ado that I thrust myself back into the workforce, ready for everything that is about to come my way. I recently graduated from an intensive data analytics program through Merit America with my Google Data Analytics Certification. I'm excited to utilize my creativity and skills to divulge the stories that data uncovers.
